How to play

Controls

Move Character With Arrow (WASD) Keys and guess if the square contains a point, to confirm placement press space (Enter). Player turns red to indicate miss, green indicates a point. total points can be seen at bottom of game screen

Score

The first player to get 3 points wins! points can be earned by correctly guessing the randomly generated point squares correctly
